## Recommended reading order

1. Start with **Auth + Hosted Verify** if your product needs the canonical hosted path.
2. Move to **NFT Gating** or **Domain Verification** for concrete end-user policy examples.
3. Use **Sybil Resistance** and **Verifiable Agents** when trust requirements become stricter.
4. Finish with **Hub Integrator Setup** when you are operationalizing app attribution, sponsorship, and partner rollout.
