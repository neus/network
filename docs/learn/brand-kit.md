# Brand Kit

For official NEUS brand assets and usage guidance, use the public brand resources in the main app and site.

## Essentials

- Preferred mark: transparent, light-blue NEUS mark.
- Preserve clear space around the mark.
- Do not distort, recolor, or apply effects to official marks.
