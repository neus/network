# Manifesto

NEUS is built on one principle: **proofs, not promises**.

## Why this exists

Digital systems still rely on unverifiable claims: ownership claims, identity claims, and authenticity claims. NEUS replaces those claims with portable, cryptographic proof receipts.

## Core commitments

- **Privacy by default**: share proof outcomes without forcing raw-data disclosure.
- **Portable verification**: verify once and reuse across apps, chains, and ecosystems.
- **Open standards**: build on public, inspectable protocols and interoperable formats.
- **Composable trust**: combine verifiers to express real policy, not one-off checks.
- **User agency**: users control what they prove and what they reveal.

## What this enables

- Verifiable content and authorship
- Sybil-resistant communities and actions
- Reusable identity and ownership checks
- Agent authenticity and accountable delegation
