**Verifier ID:** `ownership-social`

Hosted: X, Discord, GitHub, Farcaster, Telegram, etc. `VerifyGate` or `/verify`. Visibility: [Security and trust](../platform/security-and-trust).

## Best For

- Trust pages and reputation
- Community onboarding

## What You Configure

| Field | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| `provider` | Yes | Example: `github`, `discord`, `x`, or `farcaster` |
| `walletAddress` | No | Optional subject override |

User picks provider to provider auth to return via hosted flow. You get a proof id; store and reuse. Do not collect raw OAuth tokens.
