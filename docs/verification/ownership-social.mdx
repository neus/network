**Verifier ID:** `ownership-social`

Use this verifier when you need to prove that a user controls a social account such as X, Discord, GitHub, Farcaster, or Telegram.

## Starts in hosted verify

Launch hosted verify or `VerifyGate`. The user completes the account-link step in the NEUS flow.

## Best For

- social-linked trust pages
- community onboarding
- account reputation and discovery

## What You Configure

| Field | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| `provider` | Yes | Example: `github`, `discord`, `x`, or `farcaster` |
| `walletAddress` | No | Optional subject override |

## What the User Does

- Chooses the social provider
- Signs in or authorizes access through the provider
- Returns to your app through the hosted flow

## What Your App Receives

Your app receives a proof receipt ID after the hosted flow completes. Save it and reuse it for profiles, access checks, or proof display.

## Use Hosted Verify

Always route users through `/verify` or `VerifyGate` for this verifier. Do not ask users for raw OAuth tokens.
