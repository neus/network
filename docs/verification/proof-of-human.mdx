**Verifier ID:** `proof-of-human`

Use this verifier when you need a strong anti-bot or anti-sybil signal before access, payouts, or sensitive actions.

**Start here:** Hosted Verify (or `VerifyGate` with hosted checkout). Visibility: **[Security and trust](../platform/security-and-trust)**.

## Best For

- sybil resistance
- reward programs
- high-value onboarding

## Starts in hosted verify

Launch hosted verify or `VerifyGate`. The user completes the human-verification step in the NEUS flow.

## What You Configure

| Field | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| `provider` | No | Use when you need to request a specific supported provider |

## What the User Does

- Starts the hosted human-verification flow
- Completes the provider step
- Returns to your app after completion

## What Your App Receives

Your app receives a proof receipt ID that can be reused for gated actions, reward programs, or abuse reduction policies.

## Use Hosted Verify

This verifier should be launched through `/verify` or a widget-driven hosted flow.
