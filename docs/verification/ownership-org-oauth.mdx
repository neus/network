**Verifier ID:** `ownership-org-oauth`

Google or Microsoft workspace membership. Hosted `/verify` or `VerifyGate` - avoid custom OAuth UI. Visibility: [Security and trust](../platform/security-and-trust).

## Best For

- Gating to members of a Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 organization
- Org-scoped agents and apps

## Example

```text
https://neus.network/verify?preset=org-pack&returnUrl=https://app.example.com/verified
```

## What You Configure

| Field | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| `provider` | Yes | `google` or `microsoft` |
| `expectedOrgDomain` | No | Restrict the proof to one company domain such as `acme.com` |

<Tip>
`expectedOrgDomain` only when a single company domain should pass; omit if any Workspace / M365 tenant is OK.
</Tip>
