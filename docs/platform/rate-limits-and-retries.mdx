---
title: Rate Limits and Retries
description: What to do when requests are throttled or fail temporarily.
icon: clock
---

NEUS APIs may return **`429`** or short-lived errors.

- Use **exponential backoff** and respect **`Retry-After`** when the response includes it.
- When **creating** proofs, avoid blind retries — confirm whether the first request succeeded before posting again.
- For **polling**, space requests a few seconds apart and back off on errors.
