Quick reference for the verification flow.

## In Short

- Your app starts a verification request.
- The user completes the required step, such as signing, linking an account, or finishing a hosted check.
- NEUS runs the verifier and returns a proof receipt ID.
- Your app saves that proof receipt ID for future checks, proof pages, and product access.
