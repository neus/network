NEUS bills metered usage to the profile linked to your app. Credits spend first.

## What `appId` does

`appId` identifies which product made the request. It is public metadata, not a secret.

## When credits run out

Billable calls return **402** with a quote. Complete payment and retry with `PAYMENT-SIGNATURE`.

## Payment gates

Payment gates charge visitors. NEUS usage still bills through the linked profile.
