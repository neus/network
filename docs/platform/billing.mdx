NEUS bills metered verification usage to a NEUS profile. Credits spend first.

## Default: your account pays for user checks

When users verify through **VerifyGate** or **Hosted Verify**, pass both:

| Field | Role |
|-------|------|
| `appId` | Public product ID from Hub → Apps |
| `billingWallet` | Your Hub wallet — credits on this profile pay for the check |

Register the app once in Hub, then include both values in the SDK or hosted URL (`?appId=` and `?billingWallet=`). NEUS routes usage to your account so the end user is not charged for the verification step.

## What `appId` alone does

`appId` tells NEUS which product made the request. It is public metadata, not a secret. **To bill your account, you also need `billingWallet` and a completed app registration.**

## Server checks

Use the same `appId` and `billingWallet` on **`gateCheck`** so server-side eligibility reads stay aligned with your billing account.

## When credits run out

Billable calls return **402** with a quote. Complete payment and retry with `PAYMENT-SIGNATURE`.

## Payment gates

Payment gates can charge your visitors directly. NEUS metered verification usage still bills through your registered app when `billingWallet` is set.

## Advanced paths

Server-side receipt creation and per-user approvals use separate flows — see [Server integrations (advanced)](../cookbook/server-advanced).
