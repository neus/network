NEUS bills metered verification usage to a NEUS profile. Credits spend first.

## Default: your account pays for user checks

When users verify through **VerifyGate** or **Hosted Verify**, use the snippet from Hub after app registration. It already includes:

| Field | Role |
|-------|------|
| `appId` | Public product ID from your registered domain |
| `billingWallet` | Your Hub account — the wallet you signed in with when you registered |

You do not look up or copy these separately after registration. For server-side **`gateCheck`**, reuse the same values from your Hub snippet (for example as `NEUS_APP_ID` and `NEUS_BILLING_WALLET`).

## What `appId` alone does

`appId` tells NEUS which product made the request. It is public metadata, not a secret. **To bill your account, you also need the registered app and its linked billing wallet** — both are in the Hub snippet.

## Server checks

Use the same `appId` and `billingWallet` on **`gateCheck`** so server-side eligibility reads stay aligned with your billing account.

## When credits run out

Billable calls return **402** with a quote. Complete payment and retry with `PAYMENT-SIGNATURE`.

## Payment gates

Payment gates can charge your visitors directly. NEUS metered verification usage still bills through your registered app when `billingWallet` is set.

## Advanced server integration

Server-side receipt creation and per-user approvals use separate flows — see [Integrations](../cookbook/integrations).
