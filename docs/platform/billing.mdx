Start with [Get started](../get-started). This page covers **credits**, **who pays**, and **x402**.

**Default:** you configure the app in **app setup** (org credits, caps). Users see Hosted Verify or your UI, not billing internals. Self-pay and **x402**: [x402](./x402).

## `appId` and credits

**`appId`** tells NEUS which app the traffic belongs to. It **does not** move money by itself.

## Who pays

1. **Customer traffic (typical product):** pass **`sponsorGrant`** on the SDK with **`appId`** so charges debit **your** org credits. [Platform setup](./setup).
2. **No sponsor:** the **signing wallet’s** NEUS balance is used when a call is billable. Fine for development; not what you want for anonymous production volume.
3. **HTTP 402:** pay via **x402**, then retry with **`PAYMENT-SIGNATURE`**. [x402](./x402).

NEUS resolves the payer from the **request** (sponsor grant, signer, payment header). You cannot “bill by `appId`” with `appId` alone in the body.

## Who you are

| | Billing |
|--|---------|
| **Product** | Org credits + **`sponsorGrant`** so usage hits **your** balance. |
| **Solo** | Your credits (or x402). `appId` optional. |

## Bill customer traffic to your org

1. App + org credits in [app setup](./setup).
2. Short-lived **`sponsorGrant`** tied to your org.
3. `new NeusClient({ appId, sponsorGrant })` on SDK calls.

[Platform setup](./setup)

<Warning>
High-volume anonymous checks without a sponsor or session are for **dev or tiny volume** only.
</Warning>

## Individuals

1. Hosted verify or SDK with **your** wallet.
2. Your NEUS balance.
3. On **402**, complete x402. [x402](./x402)

## SDK fields

| Field | Role |
|-------|------|
| `appId` | App attribution |
| `sponsorGrant` | Bill your org for this client/session |
| `paymentSignature` | After an x402 challenge |

The SDK does not auto-pay x402: retry after payment or keep credits healthy.

## Visibility vs billing

Public or unlisted proofs can be **checked** without login. **Who can see** a proof is separate from **who pays**.
