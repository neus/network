## Stable Production Releases

- **Zero-Brittle Upgrades:** APIs are designed additively. Any structural updates follow strict SemVer major bumps.
- **Hosted Resilience:** Our distributed verification engine operates with high availability and redundant databases to ensure zero latency impact on your gates.
- **Private by Default:** Follow our [Security and trust](./security-and-trust) defaults to keep proof content localized and secure.

## Recommended for new projects

1. **Start with one path** — [Quickstart](../quickstart), [Agents](../agents/overview), or [MCP setup](../mcp/setup).
2. **Pin dependencies** — e.g. `"@neus/sdk": "^1.1.0"` and test upgrades before rolling to production.
3. **Prefer OAuth for editors** — `neus auth` for Cursor, VS Code, and Claude Code; Profile access keys for servers and CI only.
4. **Check receipts before trusted actions** — use gate checks and `neus_proofs_check` in agent workflows.
