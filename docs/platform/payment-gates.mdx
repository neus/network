**Payment gates** collect from your visitors. **NEUS usage** bills your linked profile. Credits first. Then [x402](./x402).

Publish a gate in [Platform](https://neus.network/hub). Use [Hosted Verify](../cookbook/auth-hosted-verify) and [Integration](../integration). Pass the gate id from Platform.
