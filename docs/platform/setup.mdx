Register your site in Hub before you ship **VerifyGate** or **Hosted Verify**. One signed step registers your domain, links billing to your Hub account, and gives you a paste-ready snippet.

## Steps

1. Sign in at [neus.network/hub](https://neus.network/hub).
2. Open **Apps** → **Register app**.
3. Enter your site URL and confirm once.
4. Copy the **integration snippet** Hub shows on success — or open **Apps** anytime for activity, check defaults, and copy-ready widget, hosted, and server integration.

**Apps** is your ongoing control center: attribution stats, credit usage per app, and pre-filled integration copy. You do not hunt for app IDs or wallet addresses separately.

Paste the snippet so user verifications spend **your** credits, not theirs.

```jsx
import { VerifyGate } from '@neus/sdk/widgets';

<VerifyGate
  appId="your-app-id"
  billingWallet="0xYourHubWallet"
  requiredVerifiers={['ownership-basic']}
>
  <ProtectedContent />
</VerifyGate>
```
