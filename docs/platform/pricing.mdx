NEUS is **credits-based**. Start free, then scale checks and saved receipts as your product grows.

**Hosted sign-in** for your app (`intent=login`) is included — credits apply when a **trust check runs** or a **receipt is saved** (gates, API, MCP), not when someone signs in to NEUS.

| Plan | Price | Included |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Basic** | Free | 500 credits / month |
| **Pro** | $19.99 / mo · $199.90 / yr | 50,000 credits / month |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Volume, SLAs, rollout support |

Per-action credit costs vary by check. Use the live estimator on **[neus.network/pricing](https://neus.network/pricing)** for current amounts — heavier checks (human verification, agent permissions) use more credits than a simple ownership check.
