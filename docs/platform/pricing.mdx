NEUS is **credits-based**. Start free, then scale checks and saved receipts as your product grows.

**Hosted sign-in** for your app (`intent=login`) is included — credits apply when a **trust check runs** or a **receipt is saved** (gates, API, MCP), not when someone signs in to NEUS.

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Included |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Basic** | Free | 500 credits / month |
| **Pro** | $19.99 / mo · $199.90 / yr | 10,000 credits / month |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Volume, SLAs, rollout support |

## How credits map to cost

Every action on NEUS costs **1 credit = $0.0025**. The difference is in how many credits an action requires:

- **Check an existing receipt (gate check):** 1 credit — **$0.0025** per check.
- **Create and save a new receipt (verification + persist):** 4–73 credits — **$0.01 to $0.18** depending on the verifier.

The economics favor reuse: creating a wallet-risk receipt costs $0.18, but checking it at any gate costs $0.0025 — 72× cheaper to reuse than to rebuild.

Use the live estimator on **[neus.network/pricing](https://neus.network/pricing)** for per-verifier credit amounts. Heavier checks (human verification, agent permissions, wallet risk) use more credits than a simple ownership check.
