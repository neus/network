Use this page when you need a quick production checklist for proof status, retries, reuse, and revocation.

## Lifecycle at a glance

1. **Submit a verification request** from hosted verify, SDK, or API.
2. **Track status** until the proof is complete for async verifiers.
3. **Store the proof receipt ID** (`proofId`) for future checks.
4. **Reuse the receipt** for access control and policy checks.
5. **Revoke only when needed** for policy or account changes.

## Status handling guidance

- Expect both immediate and asynchronous completions depending on verifier type.
- Poll status only while a flow is in progress.
- Use idempotent retry patterns for transient failures.
- Treat completed proof receipts as stable application records.
