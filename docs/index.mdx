NEUS is the programmable trust, authorization, and billing layer for the agentic web. Start with the fastest path for your use case, then reuse the same receipt everywhere.

## What NEUS solves

1. **Identity and access** - Prove who or what is acting.
2. **Authorization and delegation** - Prove what it can do.
3. **Billing and receipts** - Prove what was paid for and what came back.

## What you can build

| Use case | Verifier |
|----------|----------|
| Agent permissions and delegation | `agent-identity` · `agent-delegation` |
| Paid APIs and paid tools | `billing` · `x402` |
| Verified creators and publishers | `ownership-basic` |
| Token-gated products | `nft-ownership` · `token-holding` |
| Human-only access | `proof-of-human` |
| Domain-verified organizations | `ownership-dns-txt` · `ownership-org-oauth` |
