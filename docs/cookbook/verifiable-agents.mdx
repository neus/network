Two proofs: **`agent-identity`** (agent signs) and **`agent-delegation`** (approving account signs). Same person or two accounts.

To bill hosted checks through your product, complete [Quickstart](../quickstart) (**Register app** in Hub). For backend receipt creation after user approval, see [Integrations](../cookbook/integrations). Agent concepts: [Agent concepts](../agents/concepts)

## MCP

<Steps>
 <Step title="Check">
 **`neus_context`** → **`neus_agent_link`**. Done if **`linked: true`**.
 </Step>
 <Step title="Create">
 **`neus_agent_create`** when proofs are missing.
 </Step>
 <Step title="Finish">
 Complete **`hostedVerifyUrl`** or signing steps from the response.
 </Step>
 <Step title="Confirm">
 **`neus_agent_link`** until **`linked: true`**.
 </Step>
</Steps>
