`agent-identity` (agent wallet signs) and `agent-delegation` (approving account signs) are **separate** proofs. One person can do both, or roles can split. [Agent concepts](../agents/concepts)

## MCP

<Steps>
 <Step title="Create">
 `neus_agent_create`
 </Step>
 <Step title="Hosted handoff">
 Open **hostedVerifyUrl** from the response.
 </Step>
 <Step title="Link">
 `neus_agent_link` until readiness reports identity (+ delegation if configured).
 </Step>
</Steps>
