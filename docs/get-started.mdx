NEUS issues portable **proof IDs**. **Save** the id, **check** it before starting another verification, then **reuse** it on the next surface for gates and decisions. How that loop fits your stack is in [Integration](./integration).

## 1. Sign in to NEUS

[Open your account](https://neus.network/hub) with passkey or wallet. Complete your profile so **Apps** and **Credits** are available.

## 2. Link your app

[Platform setup](./platform/setup). Domain, caps, **`appId`**. Traffic ties to your billing profile.

## 3. Run your first loop

1. **Hosted checkout.** [Hosted Verify](./cookbook/auth-hosted-verify). `https://neus.network/verify`
2. **SDK.** [Quickstart](./quickstart)

Credits apply to your linked profile. Optional [x402](./platform/x402) and [visitor-side charges](./platform/payment-gates). Pricing and credits are documented under [Billing](./platform/billing).
